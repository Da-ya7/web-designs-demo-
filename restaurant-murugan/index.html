Three Generations, One Kitchen

Since 1998, Murugan Tiffin Centre has been serving authentic South Indian cuisine with love and dedication. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved institution across Chennai.

We believe in honoring traditional recipes while maintaining the highest standards of freshness and quality. Every dish is prepared with the finest ingredients and served hot with care.

Our family's commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name across Chennai. Join us for an authentic South Indian experience.
